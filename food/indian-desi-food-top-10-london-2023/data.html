London's Finest Indian Restaurants: Top 10

Published on: 02-09-2023
Published by: Admin

London is renowned for its vibrant culinary scene, offering a diverse array of international cuisines. When it comes to Indian food, the city boasts a plethora of exceptional restaurants that cater to every palate. In this article, we will embark on a gastronomic adventure through the streets of London to discover the top Indian restaurants that serve up delectable flavors, authentic dishes, and an unforgettable dining experience.

1. Dishoom:
With its stylish ambiance and a delectable menu inspired by Bombay's Irani cafes, Dishoom has become a beloved name among Indian food enthusiasts. Their culinary creations, including their famous bacon naan rolls and flavorful curries, are sure to tantalize your taste buds.

Location- Covent Garden, Shoreditch, King's Cross, and more

Website: www.dishoom.com

2. Gymkhana:
Transport yourself to the grandeur of a colonial Indian sports club at Gymkhana. This Michelin-starred restaurant offers a sophisticated dining experience, presenting elevated Indian dishes crafted with finesse and a blend of traditional and contemporary flavors.

Location- Mayfair

Website: www.gymkhanalondon.com

3. Masala Zone:
For a vibrant and casual dining experience, Masala Zone is a popular choice. Known for its street food-inspired menu, this restaurant showcases the diverse regional cuisines of India, offering a wide range of flavors and spice levels to satisfy all palates.

Location - Covent Garden, Soho, Islington, and more

Website: www.masalazone.com

4. Benares:
Immerse yourself in an elegant dining experience at Benares, a Michelin-starred restaurant known for its refined Indian cuisine. With a blend of traditional and modern flavors, their meticulously crafted dishes will take you on a culinary journey through India.

Location- Mayfair

Website: www.benaresrestaurant.com

5. Tayyabs:
If you're in search of authentic Punjabi cuisine, Tayyabs in East London is a must-visit. Renowned for its succulent tandoori dishes, fragrant biryanis, and flavorful curries, this bustling restaurant has earned a reputation for its delicious and affordable Indian fare.

Location- Whitechapel

Website: www.tayyabs.co.uk

6. Veeraswamy:
Steeped in history, Veeraswamy holds the distinction of being one of the oldest Indian restaurants in the UK. Located in the heart of London's West End, this fine dining establishment offers a refined selection of dishes inspired by the royal kitchens of India.

Location- Mayfair

Website: www.veeraswamy.com

7. Chutney Mary:
With its elegant ambiance and contemporary twist on Indian classics, Chutney Mary is a culinary gem in London's restaurant scene. Their innovative menu features a fusion of Indian flavors with global influences, delivering a unique dining experience.

Location- Chutney Mary - St. James's

Website: www.chutneymary.com

8. Amaya:
For a taste of Indian cuisine with a modern twist, Amaya combines traditional cooking techniques with contemporary presentation. The interactive grill kitchen adds an element of excitement as you witness the chefs expertly prepare tantalizing kebabs and grilled dishes.

Location- Belgravia

Website: www.amaya.biz

9. Darjeeling Express:
Founded by chef Asma Khan, Darjeeling Express is a testament to authentic home-style Indian cooking. With a focus on flavors from West Bengal, their menu celebrates traditional recipes passed down through generations, creating a truly heartwarming dining experience.

Location: Darjeeling Express - Covent Garden

Website: www.darjeeling-express.com

10. Cinnamon Club:
Situated in a stunning Grade II listed building, Cinnamon Club offers a refined take on Indian cuisine. The menu showcases inventive dishes that blend traditional flavors with a contemporary twist, all served in an elegant and stylish setting.

Location - Westminster

Website: www.cinnamonclub.com

London's Indian restaurant scene offers a diverse range of flavors and experiences, allowing diners to embark on a culinary adventure without leaving the city. From Dishoom's bustling Bombay-inspired ambiance to the elegant settings of Benares and Chutney Mary, each restaurant brings a unique touch to Indian cuisine. Whether you crave authentic street food flavors at Masala Zone or desire an elevated dining experience at Gymkhana, these top Indian restaurants in London provide an exquisite taste of India's rich culinary heritage. So, venture out and indulge in the vibrant and flavorful world of Indian cuisine right in the heart of London.
