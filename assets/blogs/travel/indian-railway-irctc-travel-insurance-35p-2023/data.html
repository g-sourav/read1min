When it comes to train travel in India, the Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) is a household name. Known for its seamless ticket booking system and convenient services, IRCTC also offers an important feature that every traveler should consider - travel insurance. In this article, we will explore the benefits and importance of IRCTC travel insurance, providing you with valuable insights to make informed decisions for a safe and secure journey.

What is IRCTC Travel Insurance?

IRCTC travel insurance is a comprehensive insurance policy designed to protect passengers against unforeseen events and mishaps during their train journey. It offers coverage for accidents, disabilities, hospitalization, and even unfortunate incidents like train derailments or accidents. With a minimal premium, passengers can enjoy peace of mind knowing that they are financially protected in case of any unfortunate circumstances.

Here are the steps on how to get IRCTC insurance when booking a train ticket:

  1. Visit the IRCTC website or app: Open the IRCTC website (https://www.irctc.co.in) or download and open the IRCTC app on your smartphone.
  2. Log in to your account or create an account if you don't have one.
  3. Select the train you want to book a ticket for.
  4. Enter the passenger details. (Name, age, id, contact info)
  5. Select the class of travel.(seat preference)
  6. Opt for travel insurance: Before proceeding to payment check the box to opt for travel insurance and read the terms and conditions of the insurance policy.
  7. Enter your payment details.
  8. Click on the "Book Ticket" button.

Once your payment is successful, you'll receive a confirmation message with your ticket details, including the PNR number. The insurance details will also be mentioned in the confirmation.

View IRCTC insurance details: To view the insurance details later, log in to your IRCTC account, go to "My Bookings," and select the respective ticket. You'll find the insurance policy number and other relevant information.

Cost and T&Cs:

IRCTC insurance costs only 35 paise per passenger. IRCTC insurance is the cheapest travel insurance available in India. The insurance covers the cost of your ticket in case of cancellation or delay. IRCTC insurance also covers the cost of your medical expenses in case of an accident.

Here are the details of the IRCTC insurance:

  • Cost: 35 paise per passenger
  • Coverage:
    • Insurance Cost of the ticket in case of cancellation or delay
    • Medical expenses in case of an accident
  • Terms and conditions of IRCTC Insurance:
    • The insurance is valid for the entire journey.
    • The insurance is not valid for travel on special trains, such as Rajdhani Express or Shatabdi Express.
    • The insurance is not valid for travel on trains that are canceled or delayed due to reasons beyond the control of the indian railways, such as bad weather or strikes.

The amount of compensation to be given to passengers is as follows

  • In case of Death - 10 lakh
  • Permanent Total Disability - 10 Lakh
  • Permanent Partial Disability - 7.5 Lakh
  • Hospitalization Expenses for Injury - 2 Lakh
  • Transportation of mortal remains 10 Thousand.

If you are planning to travel by train, I recommend purchasing IRCTC travel insurance. It can give you peace of mind and financial protection in case of any unforeseen circumstances during your train journey.
