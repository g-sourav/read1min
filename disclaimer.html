Cillian Murphy Stars in Christopher Nolan's Oppenheimer: A Gripping Look at the Man Who Made the Atomic Bomb

Published on: 02-09-2023
Published by: Admin

Oppenheimer is an upcoming biographical thriller film written and directed by Christopher Nolan, based on the 2005 book American Prometheus: The Triumph and Tragedy of J. Robert Oppenheimer by Kai Bird and Martin J. Sherwin. The film stars Cillian Murphy as J. Robert Oppenheimer, an American theoretical physicist credited with being the "father of the atomic bomb" for his role in the Manhattan Project—the World War II undertaking that developed the first nuclear weapons. The film also stars Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, Robert Downey Jr., Florence Pugh, Rami Malek, Benny Safdie, Michael Angarano, Josh Hartnett and Kenneth Branagh.

The film is set against the backdrop of World War II and follows Oppenheimer's life from his early days as a student at Harvard University to his role in the Manhattan Project. The film also explores Oppenheimer's personal life, including his relationship with his wife, Kitty, and his struggles with the moral implications of his work.

Oppenheimer is scheduled to be released on July 21, 2023 by Universal Pictures.

 

Here are some additional details about the film:

  • The film is being produced by Christopher Nolan and Emma Thomas through their production company, Syncopy.
  • The film is being shot in a combination of IMAX 65mm and 65mm large-format film—including, for the first time, sections in IMAX black-and-white analog photography.
  • The film's budget is estimated to be $100 million.
  • The film has been rated R for "graphic violence, disturbing images, and some language."

Oppenheimer is one of the most anticipated films of 2023. The film has a star-studded cast, a talented director, and a big budget. It is sure to be a critical and commercial success.
